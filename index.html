About Me

I am building a career in data science with purpose.

I am an aspiring Data Scientist currently studying Data Science with ALX Africa. I have a strong interest in Transport and Logistics Analytics because I believe data can help reduce delays, improve decision-making, and make systems more efficient.

Although I do not have formal work experience yet, I am actively growing through learning, certification, volunteer work, and practical portfolio development. I have passed both PCEP™ – Certified Entry-Level Python Programmer and PCAP™ – Certified Associate Python Programmer.

Alongside my studies, I volunteer at Young Africans Technology, where I support learners with Maths, English, coding, basic computer skills, learner records, Excel organization, data cleaning, and community activities.

I may be early in my professional journey, but I am building with purpose, discipline, and a clear direction in data science.
